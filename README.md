# MUI Theme Customizer

A visual theme builder for [Material UI](https://mui.com), inspired
by inspireUI's theme generator. Design a theme by eye — colors,
fonts, corner rounding, sizing — and watch it applied live across a
gallery of real components, no code required until you're ready to
use it.

## Getting started

Visit [~.github.io/mui-theme-customizer/](https://mansourcodes.github.io/mui-theme-customizer/) to try it
out. Start tweaking the panel on the left, and watch the preview
update as you go. Nothing is saved anywhere but your own browser, so
feel free to experiment — Reset and Randomize are always one click
away.

## What you can do

- **Pick your colors** — set the primary, secondary, and status
  colors, plus background and text colors, with a warning if any
  text becomes hard to read against its background
- **Choose a font** — search the full Google Fonts library and set
  your base text size and heading scale
- **Round the corners** — control how rounded cards, buttons, and
  form fields are, independently
- **Resize controls** — make buttons, inputs, and switches bigger or
  smaller to match your product's feel
- **See it live** — every change appears instantly on a full page of
  sample cards, forms, charts, and tables, so you always know what
  your theme actually looks like
- **Check it in Arabic/RTL** — flip a switch to preview your theme
  mirrored with Arabic text, useful if your product supports
  right-to-left languages
- **Start from a preset** — pick from a gallery of built-in themes,
  or hit Randomize for instant inspiration
- **Save your favorites** — keep your own themes around for later;
  they stay saved in your browser
- **Take it with you** — copy the finished theme as code and drop it
  straight into a Material UI project
